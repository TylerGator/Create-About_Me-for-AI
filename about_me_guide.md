# How to Create Your AI Context Profile (`about_me.md`)

This guide walks you through creating a personal context file for AI assistants. Once built, this file gives any AI chatbot an instant understanding of who you are, what you do, and how you like to work — so you don't have to re-explain yourself every session.

---

## What You'll Need

- The file `create_about_me.md` (the questionnaire template)
- Access to an AI chatbot (e.g., Claude, ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot)
- A text editor to save your finished profile (Notepad, VS Code, TextEdit, or any plain text editor)

---

## Step 1 — Fill In What You Know in `create_about_me.md`

Open `create_about_me.md` in any text editor and fill in as much as you can before your AI session.

**Focus on Tier 1 first** — these are the required fields and the most important:

- Your name, title, and department
- A brief description of your role and responsibilities
- The top tasks you'd ask an AI to help with
- Key people you work with (boss, direct reports, key peers)

You don't need to fill everything in perfectly. Incomplete answers, rough notes, and point-form thoughts are fine — the AI will help you refine them in the next step. Tier 2 and Tier 3 sections can be left blank and completed during the chat interview.

> **Tip:** Save your partially filled file before moving on. You can always come back and update it later.

---

## Step 2 — Load the File and Enter the Setup Prompt

Open your AI chatbot and start a new conversation. Upload or paste the contents of your `create_about_me.md` file, then enter the following prompt:

---

### 📋 Setup Prompt — Copy and Use This

```
I'm uploading my AI context profile template (create_about_me.md).

Please read the full file. I've filled in what I can in Tier 1.

Help me complete and refine my profile by asking me follow-up questions — 
one at a time — to fill in any gaps, clarify my answers, and work through 
Tier 2 (writing style and preferences). Ask me up to 5 refinement questions.

When we're done, generate a clean, complete about_me.md file I can copy 
and save. Write it as if briefing another AI assistant who has never met me.
```

---

The AI will read your template, review what you've filled in, and ask targeted follow-up questions to fill gaps and deepen your profile — rather than starting from scratch.

---

## Step 3 — Answer the Refinement Questions

The AI will ask up to 5 follow-up questions, one at a time. Answer them in plain language — conversational responses work just as well as polished ones.

These questions will typically focus on:

- Clarifying anything vague in your Tier 1 answers
- Your preferred writing tone and communication style (Tier 2)
- How you like information presented to you
- Recurring themes or priorities in your work
- Anything the AI needs to serve you better

There are no wrong answers. The goal is to give the AI enough context to be genuinely useful, not to produce a perfect document.

---

## Step 4 — Copy the Output into a New `about_me.md` File

When the AI finishes, it will generate a clean, completed profile. Copy the full output and paste it into a new file in your text editor. Save it as:

```
about_me.md
```

Store it somewhere easy to find — a desktop folder, a shared drive, or alongside other tools you use regularly.

> **This is your persistent memory file.** Every time you start an AI session, load this file first and the AI will immediately understand your context.

---

## How to Use `about_me.md` in Future Sessions

At the start of any AI session, upload or paste your `about_me.md` and use this prompt:

---

### 📋 Session Start Prompt — Copy and Use This

```
I'm sharing my AI context profile (about_me.md). 

Please read it and use it as your background context for our entire 
conversation. You do not need to confirm each detail back to me — 
just apply it. Let me know when you're ready.
```

---

The AI will load your profile silently and apply it to every response — matching your tone, understanding your role, and anticipating the kind of help you need.

---

## Keeping Your Profile Current — Automatic Updates

Your `about_me.md` stays useful only if it reflects how you actually work. The good news: you don't have to remember to update it yourself.

**The AI monitors your sessions automatically.** When you load `about_me.md` at the start of a session, the AI quietly watches for signals that something in your profile may have changed — a new project, a new key person, a shift in priorities, or a tone preference you expressed during the conversation. It won't interrupt your work to mention this.

**At a natural break point**, such as when you've finished a task or wrapped up a project, the AI will ask a single brief question — something like:

> *"Before we finish — you mentioned a new working group with the Office of Finance that isn't in your profile. Would you like me to suggest an amendment to capture that?"*

**If you say yes**, the AI will show you exactly what it proposes to change — the specific section, the current text, and the proposed new text — before asking for your confirmation. Once you approve it, it will give you the updated text to copy into your file, along with a change log entry.

**If you say no or not now**, it moves on without further prompting.

You can also update your profile manually at any time. Open `about_me.md` in a text editor and edit any section directly. Or load the file into an AI and say: *"I need to update section 1.3 — my priorities have shifted."*

> **Tip:** After a major project wraps up or a significant organizational change, it's worth loading your profile and asking: *"Based on what we worked on today, does anything in my profile need updating?"*

## Quick Reference

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `create_about_me.md` | The questionnaire template. Use once (or share with colleagues). |
| `about_me.md` | Your completed personal profile. Load at the start of every AI session. |

| Prompt | When to Use |
|---|---|
| **Setup Prompt** | First time — when creating your profile from the template |
| **Session Start Prompt** | Every subsequent AI session |
| **Manual Update Prompt** | Any time you want to revise a specific section yourself |

---

### 📋 Manual Update Prompt — Copy and Use This

```
I'm sharing my AI context profile (about_me.md).

Please read it, then help me update [describe the section or topic, 
e.g., "my key relationships — I have a new direct report" or 
"my top priorities — the budget cycle just ended"].

Show me the proposed change before finalizing, and give me a 
change log entry I can paste into the Profile Change Log.
```
